## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Visit the application homepage
2. Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation bar
3. Fill in your details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and number)
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for a verification link (if required)
6. Once verified, you can log in using your credentials

### Logging In
1. Click on the "Login" button in the top navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### First Steps After Login
1. Explore your dashboard to see an overview of your tasks
2. Create your first task using the "Add Task" button
3. Customize your notification preferences in the profile section

## Account Management

### Updating Your Profile
1. Click on your name/email in the top right corner
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Update your information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Theme preference (light/dark)
   - Notification settings
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Password
1. Navigate to the Profile page
2. Look for the "Change Password" section
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password twice for confirmation
5. Click "Update Password"

### Managing Notification Preferences
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Find the "Notification Preferences" section
3. Toggle email and/or browser notifications as needed
4. Click "Save Changes"

## Task Management

### Creating a Task
1. Click on the "Add Task" button (available on Dashboard and Tasks pages)
2. Fill in the task details:
   - Title (required)
   - Description (optional)
   - Priority (low, medium, high)
   - Due date (optional)
   - Tags (optional)
   - Recurrence pattern (optional)
3. Click "Create Task"

### Editing a Task
1. Find the task you want to edit
2. Click the "Edit" button on the task card
3. Update the task details as needed
4. Click "Update Task"

### Marking Tasks as Complete
1. Find the task you want to mark as complete
2. Click the checkbox next to the task title
3. The task will be visually marked as completed
4. To mark as incomplete again, click the checkbox again

### Deleting a Task
1. Find the task you want to delete
2. Click the "Delete" button on the task card
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

## Tags and Priorities

### Creating Tags
1. Go to the "Tags" page from the main navigation
2. Click "Create New Tag"
3. Enter a name for your tag
4. Optionally select a color for the tag
5. Click "Save"

### Assigning Tags to Tasks
1. When creating or editing a task
2. Click on the "Tags" section
3. Select existing tags or create new ones
4. The tags will be associated with the task

### Setting Task Priorities
1. When creating or editing a task
2. Use the priority selector to choose:
   - Low (green indicator)
   - Medium (yellow indicator)
   - High (red indicator)
3. The priority will be visually represented on the task card

## Searching and Filtering

### Searching Tasks
1. Use the search bar at the top of the tasks list
2. Type any keyword that appears in task titles or descriptions
3. The list will automatically filter to show matching tasks

### Filtering Tasks
1. Use the filter controls to filter by:
   - Status (pending, in progress, completed)
   - Priority (low, medium, high)
   - Date range (using the date picker)
2. Multiple filters can be applied simultaneously

### Sorting Tasks
1. Use the sort controls to sort by:
   - Due date (earliest first or latest first)
   - Priority (high to low or low to high)
   - Alphabetically (A to Z or Z to A)
   - Creation date (newest first or oldest first)

## Recurring Tasks

### Creating Recurring Tasks
1. When creating or editing a task, click "Enable Recurrence"
2. Choose the recurrence pattern:
   - Daily: Repeat every N days
   - Weekly: Repeat every N weeks on selected days
   - Monthly: Repeat every N months on selected day
   - Yearly: Repeat every N years on selected date
3. Set the interval (how often the pattern repeats)
4. Optionally set an end condition:
   - Never: Continue indefinitely
   - After N occurrences: Stop after a specific number of instances
   - On date: Stop after a specific date
5. Save the task

### Managing Recurring Task Exceptions
1. When viewing a recurring task instance
2. You can mark it as complete/incomplete without affecting other instances
3. To skip a specific occurrence, edit that instance and delete it

## Notifications

### Setting Up Notifications
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Find the "Notification Preferences" section
3. Enable email notifications and/or browser notifications
4. Save your preferences

### Browser Notifications
- Enabled by default when you allow browser notifications
- Will appear for tasks approaching their due date
- Can be customized in your browser settings

### Email Notifications
- Enabled in your profile notification settings
- Sent to your registered email address
- Include details about upcoming due dates

## Theme Options

### Switching Themes
1. Click the theme toggle button (usually in the top navigation bar)
2. The application will switch between light and dark themes
3. Your preference is saved automatically

### Theme Preferences
- Your theme preference is saved in your profile
- The application remembers your choice across sessions
- Themes are optimized for readability and accessibility

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### I can't log in
- Check that your email and password are correct
- Ensure your account is verified (check your email for verification link)
- Try resetting your password if you've forgotten it

#### Tasks aren't saving
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page and try again
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### Notifications aren't appearing
- Check your browser notification settings
- Verify notification preferences in your profile
- Ensure your email settings are correct for email notifications

#### The app is running slowly
- Clear your browser cache
- Close other tabs/applications to free up resources
- Contact us if performance issues continue

## FAQ

### How many tasks can I create?
There is no limit to the number of tasks you can create. However, for optimal performance, we recommend keeping your active task list manageable.

### Are my tasks private?
Yes, all your tasks are private and only accessible to you. We use secure authentication to ensure your data remains protected.

### Can I share tasks with others?
Currently, the application does not support task sharing. Each account has its own private task list.

### How do recurring tasks work?
Recurring tasks automatically generate new instances based on the pattern you define. Each instance is treated as a separate task that can be completed individually.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections, secure authentication, and regular security audits to protect your data.

### Can I use the app offline?
The application requires an internet connection to function. However, we're considering offline capabilities for future releases.

### How do I cancel my account?
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Find the "Account Management" section
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Follow the prompts to confirm
